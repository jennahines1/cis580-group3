### **Functional Requirements**

8. Notify parents of events, emergencies, and schedule updates.

3. Access medical information and emergency contact details.  
4. Message counselors, view assigned staff, and share photos.  
5. Edit enrolled children and manage account information.  

**Camp Worker/Counselor Features:**  
1. Monitor assigned campers, take attendance, and report issues.  
2. Submit completion reports for assigned tasks.  
3. Report missing supplies, repairs, or inappropriate behavior.

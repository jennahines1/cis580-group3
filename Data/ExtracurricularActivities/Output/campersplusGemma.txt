## CamperPlus System Requirements Summary

Here's a breakdown of the requirements for the CamperPlus system, categorized and summarized:

**I. Administrator Requirements:**

**A. Camper Management:**

*   **Add Campers:** Ability to add new camper records with relevant information (name, contact details, emergency contacts, medical history, etc.).
*   **Remove Campers:** Ability to remove campers from the system (likely for those no longer attending).
*   **Manage Historical Records:** Maintain and access camper records from previous years for easy reference.
*   **Manage Groups:** Create and manage groups of campers.
*   **Manage Enrollment:** Add and remove parents/guardians and campers to/from groups.
*   **Modify Camper Information:**  Update existing camper records with any changes.
*   **Delete Campers:** Remove campers from the database.

**B. Consent & Forms Management:**

*   **Upload Consent Forms:**  Ability to upload consent forms submitted by campers/parents.
*   **Track Form Submissions:** Record which camper submitted which forms.

**C. Scheduling & Activity Management:**

*   **Create Activities:** Schedule and manage activities for campers.
*   **Manage Task Scheduling:** Schedule tasks/activities for specific groups.
*   **Modify Scheduled Tasks:** Update existing tasks/events.
*   **Delete Scheduled Tasks:** Remove tasks/events from the schedule.
*   **View Group Calendars:** See event calendars filtered by specific groups.

**D. Reporting & Communication:**

*   **Generate Nametags:** Automatically create nametags.
*   **Create Feedback Forms:** Provide a channel for parents to submit feedback.
*   **Bi-Weekly Feedback Reports:** Generate reports for parents on their childâ€™s progress.
*   **Send Notifications:**  Send announcements and reminders to parents (e.g., upcoming events).
*   **Manage Emergency Information:** Store camper emergency contact information securely.
*   **Manage Parent Information:** Maintain records of parents/guardians.


**II. Parent Requirements:**

**A. Enrollment & Account Management:**

*   **Create Account:** Allow parents to create an account for online registration.
*   **Manage Child Enrollment:**  Enroll children in camp, and view/modify the enrollment status.
*   **View Payment Status:** Track payments related to camp fees.

**B. Camper Information & Activity Tracking:**

*   **View Camper Schedule:** See a schedule of activities for their child.
*   **View Assigned Counselors:** See which counselor is assigned to their child.
*   **Message Counselors:** Communicate with counselors.
*   **Share Photos:** Access photos of their child taken at camp.

**C. Form Submission & Management:**

*   **Submit Forms:**  Easily submit consent forms and other required forms.
*   **View Submitted Forms:** See a record of all forms they have submitted.

**III. Camp Worker Requirements:**

**A. Camper Management & Attendance:**

*   **View Assigned Campers:** See which campers they are responsible for.
*   **Take Attendance:** Track camper attendance.
*   **Report Inappropriate Behavior:** Report camper behavior to the administrator.
*   **Report Supply Shortages:** Report needed supplies to the administrator.
*   **Report Repairs:** Report necessary repairs to the administrator.

**B. Task Completion:**

*   **Submit Completion Reports:**  Complete and submit reports for assigned tasks.
*   **Manage Supplies:** Report shortages to the manager.

**C. Communication:**

*   **Connect with Parents:**  Ability to contact parents in case of an emergency.
